About

My objective is to develop my professional qualifications by delving into industry leading projects. At the same time, I am looking to participate in collaborative research with industrial and academic partners world-wide. My expertise and interest lie in the fields of software quality, software interoperability, and software engineering. My research mainly focuses on software quality metrics and interoperability issues regarding context alignment, data mapping, and interfaces definition languages.


I have skills for formulating and executing projects. I am a hard-working, highly self-motivated and achievement oriented professional. I also have high capability for analyzing and facing issues, adopting strategies, and incorporating new knowledge and the technical skills necessary to find solutions to problems
I am particularly proud of my ability for building collaborative networks connecting industry and academy. As evidence, I organized the second version of the Software Quality Workshop 2017 bringing together leading software companies, academic experts, and students of computer and systems engineering for sharing experiences and knowledge. I also did a research stay for eight months at the University of Bordeaux, achieving important research results in collaboration with the most representative experts of the systems interoperability topic.
